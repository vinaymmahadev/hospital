Frequently Asked Questions

Does Rite Surgery Health own any hospitals?
No, Rite Surgery Health does not own any hospitals. We are a healthcare facilitation platform that partners with a wide network of accredited hospitals and clinics to provide you with access to quality medical care. Our focus is on connecting you with the best facilities and doctors for your specific needs.
How does Rite Surgery make surgeries more affordable for me?
Rite Surgery helps make surgeries more affordable through several mechanisms:
  • Negotiated Rates: We leverage our network and relationships with hospitals to negotiate competitive pricing on your behalf.
  • Transparent Pricing: We provide you with clear, upfront cost estimates, helping you avoid hidden fees.
  • Financial Guidance: We can assist you in understanding different payment options, including EMI facilities and insurance benefits, to manage your expenses effectively.
  • Package Deals: Often, we can facilitate access to comprehensive surgery packages that include various services at a bundled rate, offering better value.
Do I have to pay any fees for using Rite Surgery‚Äôs services?
No, Rite Surgery's core facilitation services for finding doctors, hospitals, and getting initial consultations are generally free for patients. We aim to make healthcare access seamless and burden-free. Any charges related to the medical procedures themselves will be clearly communicated by the hospital or clinic.
How does Rite Surgery find the best doctor and hospital for me?
Rite Surgery employs a multi-faceted approach to recommend the best doctor and hospital:
  • Detailed Needs Assessment: We start by understanding your specific medical condition, preferences, location, and budget.
  • Vast Network: We have a comprehensive database of highly-rated doctors and accredited hospitals across various specialties.
  • Expert Matching: Our healthcare experts analyze your needs against our network, considering factors like doctor's specialization, experience, patient reviews, hospital facilities, and success rates.
  • Personalized Recommendations: We provide you with a curated list of options, allowing you to make an informed decision with all the necessary information.
How can Rite Surgery help me once I find my hospital and doctor of choice?
Once you've selected your hospital and doctor, Rite Surgery continues to support you by:
  • Appointment Scheduling: We help you book appointments and manage your medical calendar.
  • Pre-operative Assistance: We can guide you through any pre-operative instructions, tests, or documentation required.
  • Logistics Support: If needed, we can assist with travel and accommodation arrangements, especially for out-of-town patients.
  • Seamless Coordination: We act as a liaison between you and the hospital, ensuring smooth communication and addressing any queries.
  • Post-operative Follow-up: We may offer support for follow-up appointments and general guidance during your recovery period.
Will Rite Surgery help me file my insurance claim?
Yes, Rite Surgery can absolutely assist you with your insurance claim process. Our team can:
  • Provide Documentation Guidance: Help you understand what documents are required for your claim.
  • Facilitate Communication: Act as a bridge between you and the hospital's insurance desk to ensure all necessary paperwork is submitted correctly.
  • Answer Queries: Guide you through common insurance questions and procedures.
  • While we don't directly process claims, we empower you with the information and support needed to navigate the insurance landscape efficiently.
